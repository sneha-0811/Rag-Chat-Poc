Expense Policy

Employees may claim reimbursement for reasonable business expenses incurred during work duties.
All expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred.
Receipts are mandatory for any single expense above $50.

Travel Expenses:
Domestic business trips are covered up to $500 per trip including transport and accommodation.
International travel requires prior approval from the department head and CFO.
Economy class flights are the standard for all business travel unless the flight exceeds 6 hours.
Employees may book business class for flights longer than 6 hours with manager approval.

Meal Allowance:
Domestic travel meal allowance is $30 per day.
International travel meal allowance is $60 per day.
Client entertainment meals must be pre-approved and are capped at $100 per person.

Accommodation:
Hotel bookings should not exceed $150 per night for domestic travel.
International hotel bookings should not exceed $250 per night.

Other Expenses:
Mobile phone bills are reimbursed up to $50 per month for employees on a mobile plan agreement.
Home internet reimbursement is $30 per month for approved remote workers.
Conference and training fees require manager approval before registration.

All claims are subject to finance team review and approval before reimbursement is processed.
