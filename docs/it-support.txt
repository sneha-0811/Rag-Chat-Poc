IT Support Policy

All IT support requests must be raised via the helpdesk portal at helpdesk.company.com.
Critical issues affecting business operations will be responded to within 1 hour.
Standard requests are resolved within 2 business days.

Hardware:
Laptops are provided to all full-time employees upon joining.
Laptop replacements are approved after 3 years of usage or in case of hardware failure.
Employees are responsible for the safekeeping of company hardware.
Lost or damaged equipment must be reported to IT within 24 hours.

Software:
All software installations must be approved by the IT department.
Submit a software request via the helpdesk portal with a business justification.
Unlicensed software is strictly prohibited on company devices.

Access and Accounts:
New employee account setup is completed within 1 business day of joining.
VPN access must be requested through the IT department with manager approval.
Password resets can be performed via the self-service portal at password.company.com.
Employees must use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.

Remote Work:
Employees working remotely must connect via company VPN for accessing internal systems.
Company data must not be stored on personal devices or unauthorized cloud storage.

Security:
Phishing or suspicious emails must be reported to security@company.com immediately.
USB drives and external storage devices are not permitted on company devices without IT approval.
All company laptops have endpoint protection software installed and must not be disabled.
