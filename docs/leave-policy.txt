Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per calendar year.
Annual leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in advance via the HR portal.
Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days to the next year.

Sick leave is capped at 10 days per year. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.
Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year.

Maternity leave is 16 weeks fully paid for eligible employees.
Paternity leave is 2 weeks fully paid.

Public holidays are separate from annual leave and are not deducted from the leave balance.
Employees working on public holidays are entitled to a replacement day off.

Emergency leave of up to 3 days may be granted for immediate family bereavement.
All leave approvals are subject to manager discretion and business requirements.
